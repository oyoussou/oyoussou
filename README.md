Cloud Architect | DevOps Engineer | Scrum Master
------------------------------------------------

A seasoned Cloud Architect with expertise across major cloud platforms including AWS, GCP, and Azure. Over a year now, I’ve designed and deployed scalable, secure, and cost-effective cloud solutions for various enterprises, helping them optimize their infrastructure and embrace the cloud-first model. Most recently, I’ve broadened my skill set by earning my Scrum Master certification, which has given me a deep understanding of Agile principles and how they can drive efficiency and collaboration within development teams. With this new certification, I’m well-equipped to foster Agile practices in cloud and DevOps environments, ensuring that teams remain adaptable and focused on delivering high-quality software. As part of my transition into the DevOps space, I’m focusing on automating processes, streamlining continuous integration and deployment (CI/CD) pipelines, and enhancing collaboration between development and operations teams. I’m passionate about using infrastructure-as-code (IaC), containerization, and cloud-native tools to build resilient, automated systems that support both speed and reliability. With my background in cloud architecture, Agile leadership, and a growing focus on DevOps, I aim to create seamless, end-to-end solutions that drive business value, improve operational efficiency, and accelerate innovation.
